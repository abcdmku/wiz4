Incantations (keys)

  • ←→ walk
  • Space / Ctrl jump
  • ↑ enter door
  • ↓ duck (& vanish, once learned)
  • P pause  M mute  F2 rebind

The Spell Ladder

Fill the flask with potions to learn each spell — and mind that dying forgets them all.

  1. Higher Jump
  2. Invisibility — duck to use; you cannot move
  3. Double Jump — press jump again at the top
  4. Flame Resistance
  5. Helper Sprite — banishes creatures in your path
  6. Restore — re-learn everything after death, once

Traveller's Notes

  • Every creature is fatal to touch. The wizard does not fight — he avoids.
  • Keys open locked doors; doors travel in pairs and remember you.
  • Levers toggle the gold rune-blocks.
  • Springboards mean somewhere above is worth visiting. So do suspicious gaps.
  • Stars are worth 1000. Hidden rooms exist.
